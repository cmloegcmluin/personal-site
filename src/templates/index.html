Riding Muni down a steep hill. This tiny triangular wedge edge of a platform we're passing is my last chance to hop off unless I want to walk all the way back up this hill (which is steep enough that I may not even be able to climb it). I take a shot at it but miss. My ankle snags on top while my torso gets towed under the train as it moves along. I get run over and die.
My sister's bedroom's bathroom is a bit odd in that it has a moat. It's like a foot wide, a couple inches deep, just standing water around the edges of the room.
I'm walking up to the public restrooms. One door has a huge line so naturally I go in the one with no line. The concierge inside clears her throat at me but I still don't take the hint; it's not until I after I've stubbed my toe at the urinals and start frantically searching for a place to take off my socks to assess whether I've broken the skin and need to get my toe amputated to stop the spread of piss diseases that I realize.... everyone in my way is female... I've gone into the women's restroom! No wonder it had the shorter line! Well, anyway, I'm not bleeding, but this is going to be one hell of a welt.
